About this site

“If we do everything that’s comfortable,
then what we come up with is just kind of
a boring, comfortable result.”  

 

This site presents and documents past and current projects, while maintaining an ever-growing repository of gathered information, knowledge, memories and ideas, or any other sensible data which could potentially be proven inspiring or useful.  


VISUALS contains a curated list of digital art projects, along with some documentation regarding their concept and/or the techniques employed. AUDIO maintains a comprehensive list of music releases and still-pending-release audio snippets and sketches along with a log of live performances. MEMEX provides thorough public collection of personal notes, worth-keeping bookmarks, logging of projects, a ledger of items, and thus, constitutes a tool; as a knowledgeable, as a logging system, or merely an opportunity to hindsight and spark new ideas.
